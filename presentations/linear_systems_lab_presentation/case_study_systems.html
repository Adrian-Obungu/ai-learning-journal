Case Study: Ill-Conditioned & Singular Systems

Ill-Conditioned Systems

Systems where small input perturbations lead to massive solution variance. Characterized by a high Condition Number.

A_ill = np.array([[1.000, 2.000],
              [1.001, 2.001]])
cond = np.linalg.cond(A_ill)
Implications for AI
  • Unstable gradients during backpropagation.
  • Poor convergence in deep neural networks.
  • Necessitates feature scaling & regularization.

Singular Systems

Systems with no unique solution (inconsistent or dependent). The coefficient matrix is non-invertible (Det = 0).

A_singular = np.array([[1, 1],
                  [1, 1]])
# Parallel lines: No intersection
Implications for AI
  • Multicollinearity in regression models.
  • Redundant features causing overfitting.
  • Requires Dimensionality Reduction (PCA).
