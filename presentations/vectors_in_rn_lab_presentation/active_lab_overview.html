Active Lab: Vector Operations & Span in Python

Lab Purpose & Objectives

This Active Lab bridges the theoretical understanding of vectors to practical implementation. It's designed to solidify your grasp of vector operations and the concept of 'span' through hands-on coding.

  • Implement fundamental vector addition and scalar multiplication.
  • Develop functions to determine if a vector lies within the 'span' of others.
  • Explore different complexity levels for solving linear systems related to span.
  • Connect abstract mathematical concepts to real-world AI and cybersecurity applications.
