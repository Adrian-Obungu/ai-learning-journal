The Concept of Span: Reach of Data

What is the Span of Vectors?

The span of a set of vectors is the collection of all possible linear combinations that can be formed from those vectors.

It represents the entire subspace (line, plane, or higher-dimensional space) that the vectors can Span{v1, v2, ..., vk} vectors can generate.

Geometric Interpretation

Span of One Vector

A single non-zero vector spans a line through the origin.

Span of Two Non-Collinear Vectors

Two non-collinear vectors span a plane through the origin.

Span of N Linearly Independent Vectors

N linearly independent vectors in RN span the entire RN space.
