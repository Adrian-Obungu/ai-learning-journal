Learn Along Protocol: Join the Journey

Build in Public, Learn Together

This AI Engineering Learning Journal is more than just a personal log; it's an invitation to a community of learners. By making this repository public, we aim to foster collaborative learning and accelerate collective growth.

How to Participate:

  • **Fork the Repository**: Create your own copy of the `ai-learning-journal` on GitHub.
  • **Follow the Zettelkasten**: Utilize the structured notes for deep understanding of concepts.
  • **Engage with Active Labs**: Implement the Python labs, experiment with complexity levels, and explore real-world applications.
  • **Contribute & Share**: Open issues, suggest improvements, or share your own insights and extensions.

Whether you're a cybersecurity professional, an aspiring AI engineer, or simply curious, your journey can inspire others. Let's demystify AI together!
