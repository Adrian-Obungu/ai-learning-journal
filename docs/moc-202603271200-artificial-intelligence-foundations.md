# Map of Content - Artificial Intelligence Foundations

## Overview
This Map of Content (MOC) serves as a structured entry point into the foundational mathematical concepts underpinning Artificial Intelligence, with a particular focus on linear algebra. It synthesizes knowledge from the University of Maryland's Math 240 course, providing a comprehensive and interlinked understanding of linear equations, systems, matrices, and their applications in AI.
